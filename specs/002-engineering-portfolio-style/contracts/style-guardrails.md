# Style Guardrails Contract

## Required Tone
- Calm, premium, technical
- Modern without trend-chasing
- Editorial clarity with structured scanning rhythm

## Must-Not Rules
- No generic portfolio-template look
- No loud gradient hero as primary identity
- No purple-heavy AI branding aesthetic
- No glassmorphism overload
- No excessive scroll effects or bouncing animations
- No crowded equal-weight card grids as the dominant layout pattern

## Required Layout Behaviors
- Generous vertical spacing
- Strong section separation
- Readable text widths
- Asymmetric but balanced compositions

## Required Typography Behaviors
- Serif-led hero and section headings
- Sans-serif body and navigation
- Mono usage only for technical metadata and labels

## Required Motion Behaviors
- Soft fade/slide reveals
- Gentle hover states
- Motion used for orientation, not spectacle
